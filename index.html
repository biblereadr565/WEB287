Hi, I am Ben Harris.

I have worked in IT at SAS Software since 2015. Up until 2019 I was a Technical Intern in the Repair Depot. The Repair Depot is where all our assets/machines would come after our employees finished using them or if they were broken. Usually there was two interns in the Depot year-round that would test and fix the devices to make them deployable once again. At the end of 2019 in August I was hired as a full-time employee and I started to work on the Deskside support team. The Deskside support team had 3-4 people that would rotate on an on-call schedule to run around campus helping clients maintaining our client infrastructure. In mid 2020 I shifted towards working from home developing applications and automating tasks for my group.
